Problem Description:
Write a program to print a diamond pattern of asterisks (*) with a given number of rows.

Input: n = 5
Output:
*
***
*****
*******
*********
*******
*****
***
*

Explanation:
The pattern consists of two parts: the upper half and the lower half. The upper half has n rows, 
and the lower half has n-1 rows. Each row contains spaces followed by asterisks.
